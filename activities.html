Town hall.meetomg with deaf women at Yola, Adamawa State. 17th October 2020

Potiskum is one of the old Local Governments under Borno before Yobe State was curved out of Borno State. Today, SAVWIN paid a visit to the deaf community in Potiskum town and had a an interactive session with over 60 deaf women who trooped out to just have a glance of the Executive Director of SAVWIN. Secondary School boys were not left out as they graced the occasion to the latter. The interaction went well, they learnt a lot, SAVWIN took note of their needs, complains and demands and promised to organise programed tailored to their needs. Thier joy know no bound as they expressed it openly and promised to work with SAVWIN at all time. Thanks to the chairman and his team for making this a success. Happy to come to the ancient town of Potiskum where I did my SSS1-3.
