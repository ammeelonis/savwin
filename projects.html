WILD Nigeria
Statement of Executive Director:

The month of May 31st 2022, I left Nigeria for a program organized by Mobility International USA- MIUSA. I landed at Eugune, Oregon in USA and met beautiful women with disabilities from different countries who came for the International Institute On Women Leadership and Disability (WILD). We experienced and learnt alot within the 18 Days spent there. It was fun, it was learning new things, it was facing our fear head on to overcome it, it was building our confidence to be bold, passionate and loud.
